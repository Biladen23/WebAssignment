Our Story

Bilal Habeshan Kitchen celebrates the vibrant food and hospitality of the Habesha tradition. We combine authentic recipes passed down through generations with fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes full of spice, warmth, and heart.

  • Handmade Injera — traditional teff sourdough.
  • Signature Stews — Doro Wat, Misir Wot, and Shiro.
  • Coffee Ceremony — experience Bunna the traditional way.
